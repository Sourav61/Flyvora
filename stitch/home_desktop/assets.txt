# Flyvora Homepage Assets & Icons Specification

## 1. Pre-Footer Section: "Travel with Confidence"
This section uses high-quality SVG-style icons with a soft tonal background to communicate trust and service.

### Asset 1: Trusted Security Icon
*   **Icon Name:** `shield` (Material Symbols)
*   **Color:** Primary Blue (`#1A73E8`)
*   **Container Style:** Rounded square with a soft blue tint (`bg-blue-50` or `rgba(26, 115, 232, 0.1)`)
*   **Context:** Used for "Trusted Security" feature card.

### Asset 2: 24/7 Concierge Icon
*   **Icon Name:** `support_agent` or `headset_mic` (Material Symbols)
*   **Color:** Muted Teal (`#2EC4B6`)
*   **Container Style:** Rounded square with a soft teal tint (`bg-teal-50` or `rgba(46, 196, 182, 0.1)`)
*   **Context:** Used for "24/7 Concierge" feature card.

### Asset 3: Intelligent Routing Icon
*   **Icon Name:** `auto_awesome` or `insights` (Material Symbols)
*   **Color:** Warm Orange/Coral (Secondary accent)
*   **Container Style:** Rounded square with a soft coral tint.
*   **Context:** Used for "Intelligent Routing" feature card.

## 2. Footer: "Connect" Section Icons
The social icons in the footer are designed for a minimalist, premium look.

### Icon 1: Threads/Social Alternative
*   **Icon Name:** `alternate_email` (Material Symbols)
*   **Style:** Minimalist circle outline.

### Icon 2: Global/Web

## 3. Styling Guidelines for Developers
To replicate the look in code, use the following Tailwind-inspired utility classes:

*   **Icon Containers:** `w-12 h-12 rounded-xl flex items-center justify-center mb-6`
*   **Social Icons:** `w-10 h-10 border border-outline-variant/30 rounded-full flex items-center justify-center text-on-surface-variant hover:text-primary hover:border-primary transition-all duration-300`
*   **Font Family:** Manrope (as defined in the Horizon Ethos Design System).
